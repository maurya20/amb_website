Ambition Technologies

In partnership with technology leaders worldwide, Ambition Technologies offers an ecosystem of highly integrated Electronics design software and hardware solutions, tools and products for the Indian market. These products range from EDA Tools For PCB Design, VLSI- IC design, EM Simulation, Equipments for PCB prototyping, PCB Fabrication Product solutions, Developments Boards for IOT and LORA RF solutions, Electronics Instruments and Hardware Subsystems.

AMBITION UNIVERSITY PROGRAM

Ambition University Program (AUP), is Ambition Technologies' initiative to empower engineering education by providing the academia, easy and immediate, access to trend setting technologies in electronics systems design and development. The AUP program offers affordable but high quality, industry standard, hardware and software learning tools along with certified student and faculty training programs.
